About Nairobi

Nairobi is the capital and largest city of Kenya. It is a major economic hub in East Africa and an important center for business and commerce. Nairobi is known for its national parks, including Nairobi National Park, which is home to a variety of wildlife. The city offers a mix of urban development and natural beauty.
