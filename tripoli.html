About Tripoli

Tripoli is the capital and largest city of Libya. It is located on the northern coast of the country, overlooking the Mediterranean Sea. Tripoli is known for its ancient landmarks, such as the Red Castle Museum and the Arch of Marcus Aurelius. It offers a blend of history, culture, and stunning coastal views.
