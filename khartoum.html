About Khartoum

Khartoum is the capital of Sudan and the second-largest city in the country. It is located at the confluence of the Blue Nile and White Nile rivers. Khartoum is a vibrant city with a rich cultural heritage, offering historical sites, museums, and natural beauty. It serves as an important political and economic center in Sudan.
